# Heat Pump Manurfacturers

## Remote Assistance

We believe the heat pump remote monitoring (which we prefer to call "remote assistance") has a big role to play in improving the delivery of heat pumps across social housing, as well as saving social land lords considerable cost in the form of reduced call outs.

For this reason we have been carrying out a review of the different remote assistance technologies provided by heat pump manurfactuers. This has been collated to produce the remote assistance disemination material that can be found on the resources page.

We have tried to contact as many heat pump manurfacturers, or their party heat pump remote monitoring compainies as we can, but we are aware that there are others out there that we have not yet been able to talk to.

If you are a heat pump manurfacturer that would be like to contriubute to this project, and get your remote assistance offering included in our material, then please complete the webform that is linked to below. We will then try and get in touch to arrange an interview.
